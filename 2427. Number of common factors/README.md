# Common Factors

## Problem Statement

Given two positive integers `a` and `b`, return the number of common factors of `a` and `b`.

An integer `x` is a common factor of `a` and `b` if `x` divides both `a` and `b`.

---

## Examples

### Example 1

**Input:**
```text
a = 12, b = 6
```

**Output:**
```text
4
```

**Explanation:**
The common factors of 12 and 6 are 1, 2, 3, and 6.

---

### Example 2

**Input:**
```text
a = 25, b = 30
```

**Output:**
```text
2
```

**Explanation:**
The common factors of 25 and 30 are 1 and 5.

---

## Constraints

```text
1 <= a, b <= 1000
```
